The best Pasta Salad ever!

NOODLE SALAD??

Yes, but actually quite delicious! Dont be fooled,
pasta salad is an excellent side dish sure to please
at any event you bring it to. Its simple, yet adds a lot of dynamics to a menu.

Lets Get Started!

Ingredients

The ingredients you'll need are:
  • Pasta Noodles (any kind is okay, macaroni/rotini are great!
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • White wine vinegar
  • Mayonaisse, Salt, Pepper
  • Carrots, Olives, Green Peppers, Red Peppers

Steps

  1. Start by boiling your noodles for around
    10-15 minutes until desired softness (softer is often better)

  2. While boiling, make your sauce (in the next step)
    If your noodles finish first, strain the refridgerate

  3. Combine 2 parts white wine vinegar, 2 parts mayo,
    and 5 parts olive oil until thoroughly blended

  4. Taste it and add mayo for more creaminess
    white wine vinegar for more flavor

  5. Combine the sauce with the noodles and thorughly mix, add pepper and salt to taste

  6. Take your veggies, dice them and add them to your pasta salad, mix thoroughly

  7. Refrigerate and let cool completely, ENJOY!~
